How to Reheat The Pizza Cupcake for the Best Taste

  • Oven (Recommended): Preheat to 350°F (175°C), bake for 10–12 minutes until warm and crisp.
  • Air Fryer: 350°F for 2–3 minutes for a perfectly crispy finish.
  • Microwave: 30–45 seconds on medium power. (Quick but softer texture)

Best Ways to Store The Pizza Cupcake

  • Refrigeration: Keep in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
  • Freezing: Wrap individually and freeze for up to 3 months. Thaw overnight for best results.

Are Pizza Cupcakes Healthy?

Each Pizza Cupcake is crafted with real cheese, high-quality dough, and fresh ingredients. While they’re a delicious indulgence, they also contain a balanced mix of carbs, protein, and fats. A typical cupcake has about 220–250 calories and 12–14g of protein.
