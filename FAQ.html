Frequently Asked Questions: Your Guide to The Pizza Cupcake

Welcome to our FAQ page! Here, we’ve gathered everything you need to know about The Pizza Cupcake. Whether you’re curious about our ingredients, reheating instructions, or how to best enjoy this delicious snack, you’ll find all the answers here.

How Do I Cook The Pizza Cupcake?

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place frozen cupcakes on a baking sheet (no thawing needed).
  3. Bake for 10–12 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
  4. Let them cool for 1–2 minutes before serving.

Tip: For a crispier crust, use an air fryer at the same temp for 8–10 minutes.

Can I Microwave The Pizza Cupcake?

Yes, though baking is recommended for best texture. Microwave 1–2 cupcakes on a plate for 1–2 minutes. Expect a softer crust.

What Are the Ingredients?

  • Dough: Brioche-Neapolitan style blend.
  • Tomato Sauce: Vine-ripened tomatoes with herbs.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella and parmesan mix.
  • Toppings: Pepperoni, mushrooms, veggies (varies by flavor).

Yes! They're portion-controlled and made from real ingredients. A balanced snack better than typical fast food or frozen meals.

How Do I Store Them?

  • Freezer: Keep sealed for up to 3 months.
  • Fridge: Store leftovers up to 3 days. Reheat before enjoying.

Can I Customize Pizza Cupcakes?

Yes! Add your favorite herbs, spices, or pair with dips like ranch, garlic butter, or marinara. Perfect for a DIY pizza bar at parties.
